Miso Soup

Ingridients

  • Miso paste
  • Scallions
  • Soft tofu
  • Mushrooms
  • Spinach or kelp

Steps

  1. Heat up 1 1/2 cups of water
  2. Cut vegetables and tofu and put them into water
  3. Once everything is cooked, kill the heat and let the soup cool down a little bit
  4. Once it is warm, put a tablespoon of miso into the soup and mix well
  5. Serve
